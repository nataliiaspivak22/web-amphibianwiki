Welcome to Amphibian Wiki!

Amphibian Wiki is your ultimate resource for information about amphibians. Whether you're a nature enthusiast, a student, or just curious about these fascinating creatures, you'll find everything you need to know right here.

From frogs and toads to salamanders and newts, we cover a wide range of amphibian species from around the world. Explore our articles to learn about their habitats, behaviors, adaptations, conservation status, and more.

Our mission is to educate and inspire people to appreciate and protect amphibians and their habitats. Join us in our journey to discover the amazing world of amphibians!

Habitat Description
Forests Moist environments with ample vegetation, providing shelter and breeding grounds for amphibians.
Wetlands Marshes, swamps, and bogs that are rich in water and support diverse amphibian populations.
